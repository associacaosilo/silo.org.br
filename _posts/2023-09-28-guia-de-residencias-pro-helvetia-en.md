We are in the Guide: Residencies in South America, published by the Swiss public foundation **Pro Helvetia**, alongside other incredible artistic residency programs and spaces scattered throughout the continent!

"There are those embedded in the dynamics of urban centers, others that connect to rural territories or address diverse knowledge, both human and non-human. These projects seek to respond to local realities, while also opening space for exchange, research, and creation – in a scenario where funding for the arts is historically scarce. In Pro Helvetia's residency program, we collaborate with various partners, working across diverse disciplines and formats."
