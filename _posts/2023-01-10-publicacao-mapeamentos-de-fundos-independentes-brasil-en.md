Conducted by the Network for Philanthropy for Social Justice (RFJS), in partnership with ponteAponte, this 1st mapping presents a survey of thematic funds and independent community foundations that work with donations to civil society groups and movements active in the areas of social justice and community development in the country.

Silo - Arte e Latitude Rural is one of the 31 organizations and initiatives mapped, and the initial conclusions of the research were published in an article on the Network's blog.
