Flavia Pogliani is an economist from USP with postgraduate degrees in Marketing from Raffles College (AUS), Strategic Management from FIA-USP (BR) and Financial Planning from Nossa Escola de Planejadores (BR), and an extension in Politics & International Trade from LSE (UK). She is a businesswoman (retail and wholesale), consultant, mentor and speaker, with experience in the Third Sector (Associação Prato Cheio) and more than 30 years of experience in the private market.
